Biography

James Ning is a student at the University of Berkeley School of Information. He is currently a master's student in the MIMS program. Outside of his professional and academic endeavors, he is an avid music listener and record collector. He loves all genres of music, but he especially enjoys rock music and it's subgenres from the 1970s and 1980s. This website showcases a few of his favourite albums.
